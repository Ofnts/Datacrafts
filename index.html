ABOUT US

Datacrafts Botswana Pty LTD was established and began its business operation in 2021 providing ICT products and services, Security Products, Security Services and wireless , radio and broadband IT solutions to international and local organizations. The Company has been providing Computer Hardware and Software, ICT Consultancy , Physical Security (CCTV, Fire Alarm, Access control, Electric Fence & Motorized Gates), Hosting (Cloud, Email, Web), Network & Networking Security, Wireless Solutions, Cyber Security Solutions, Penetration Testing in Botswana ever since inception. We are focusing exclusively in high quality and cost-effective ICT solutions in all areas. We are advancing on a tremendous pace and with involvement of skilled and experienced personal and continually train staff members to keep abreast with latest technologies.
Datacrafts Botswana offers variety of ICT equipment including servers, computers, computer accessories and services by sourcing from local and international market.
